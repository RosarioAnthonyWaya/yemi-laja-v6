About This Service

Vision Into Reality.

Yemi Laja is a theatre actor, theatre producer, playwright, and creative entrepreneur with over 10 years of experience directing and producing creative work across the UK and Nigeria. She brings the same standard of excellence she holds herself to every project she leads — regardless of scale.

Whether you need a music video directed, a theatre production produced, a live event staged, or a brand narrative shaped through film and media — Yemi brings creative intelligence, purposeful vision, and hands-on execution to every project.

She is a valued member of the Crescent Theatre in Birmingham, one of the UK's longest-standing amateur theatres — and her work spans original plays, large-scale church productions, and commercial creative direction.
