About Yemi

Artist First. Everything
Around Her Is Organised.

"I don't just create — I build spaces where faith meets culture, and talent finds its voice."

Yemi Laja is a London-based worship artist, songwriter, theatre actor, vocal coach, and the founder of Mastercraft Global Limited. A multipotentialite with over 10 years of experience, she has performed on high-profile stages across Nigeria, the United Kingdom, and Ireland — and is a valued member of the renowned Crescent Theatre in Birmingham.
