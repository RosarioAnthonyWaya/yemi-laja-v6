Spoken Word

Words That Move

"Every word I speak is a declaration — not just poetry, but prophecy."

Before the songs, there were words. Spoken word has always been a core part of Yemi's creative expression — weaving together scripture, culture, and personal narrative into pieces that cut to the heart.

Her self-written spoken word pieces include:

  • ✦The King In You — Performed at CCI London, June 2024
  • ✦My Resurrection Story — Performed at Celebration Place, London, March 2024
  • ✦A Tale of Endless Grace — Performed at Vue Cinema, Westfield Mall, March 2025

Theatre

Stage Performances

Yemi Laja is a trained theatre actor, playwright, and producer — and a valued member of the renowned Crescent Theatre in Birmingham, one of the UK's longest-standing theatres.

  • "The Importance of Being Earnest" — Production Assistant & Props Manager, Crescent Theatre, Birmingham (June–July 2025)
  • "A Tale of Endless Grace" — Self-Written & Performed, Vue Cinema, Westfield Mall, London (March 2025)
  • "The King is Coming" — Lead Actor/Narrator, Ozone Cinema, Surrey Quays, London (Dec 2023)
  • "Parable of the Sower" — Lead Actor/Narrator, Phoenix Academy, London (May 2024)
  • "Ony'eze" — Lead Actor, Covenant Place, National Theatre, Lagos (May 2021)
  • "Saviour" — Self-Written, Directed & Produced, Durham UK (Dec 2022)
