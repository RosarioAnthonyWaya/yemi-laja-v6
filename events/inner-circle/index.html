About the Programme

A Space to Become Whole.

"This is not just a programme. It is a space of deep encounter, honest conversation, and lasting transformation."

Inner Circle is a 3-month virtual coaching experience for ladies who are ready to build intimacy with God, experience God's love, discover themselves, and their purpose. It is personally led by Yemi Laja and is an intimate transformative journey designed to help ladies encounter God, get better in their walk with God deeply through His word, worship, confession, and prayer. It is where women learn the Art and Act of Confessions to create their desired future.

Whether you're at a crossroads, feeling stuck, or simply hungry for more, Inner Circle meets you exactly where you are.

Secure Your Spot

What We'll Cover

Three Pillars

01
Becoming More Like Jesus

A deep dive into identity rooted in Christ — shifting from performance to presence, from striving to surrender. Practical sessions on character, virtue, and spiritual formation.

02
Building Intimacy with God

Practices and encounters that deepen your prayer life, strengthen your sensitivity to the Holy Spirit, and anchor you in a genuine personal relationship with God.

03
Fulfilling Your Purpose

Discovering who God made you to be — and building the confidence, clarity, and courage to walk fully into it. Practical tools for purpose, wholeness, and empowerment.
