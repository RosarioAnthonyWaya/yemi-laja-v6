About the Webinar

Learn from Someone Who Has Been Through It.

"I am a UK Global Talent myself — and I have supported every applicant I've worked with to a successful endorsement. I want to share what I know."

Yemi Laja is a UK Global Talent in the Arts & Culture category and has achieved a 100% success rate supporting others through the endorsement process. In this free webinar, she opens the curtain on everything — who qualifies, what evidence you need, how to structure your application, and the most common mistakes to avoid.

Whether you're just starting to explore the Global Talent route or already preparing your application, this webinar will give you clarity, direction, and confidence to take the next step.
